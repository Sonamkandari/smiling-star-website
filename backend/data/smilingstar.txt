About Us
Who We Are 
Smiling Star Private Limited is a purpose‑led technology company dedicated to building technology for social good. We focus on creating accessible, scalable, and sustainable digital solutions  for social impact that help organizations maximize their social and environmental impact.
Our team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of social challenges, ensuring every solution is designed for real users, long‑term sustainability, and measurable outcomes.
